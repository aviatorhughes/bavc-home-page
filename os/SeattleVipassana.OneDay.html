One-Day Old Student Vipassana Meditation Course


The Seattle Vipassana Hall (SVH), on behalf of the NW Vipassana Center (Dhamma Kuñja) is hosting monthly one-day courses for old students who have completed at least one ten-day course with S.N. Goenka or his assistant teachers. One-day courses are an ideal way to refresh your practice. One-day courses are typically conducted by an assistant teacher who is available to answer meditator's questions during lunch interviews. When an assistant teacher is not available to conduct the course, an authorized old student will play one-day course instructions and there are no interviews to clarify meditators' questions.

One-day courses at the SVH are held on the First Saturday of each month.

As with all Vipassana courses, donations are the only source of funding.

  • Prior registration is not required.
  • Floor mats are provided. A few cushions, chairs, and meditation benches are available to borrow.
  • Bring a vegetarian lunch. A small refrigerator is available for use.
  • Please arrive on time so that you do not disturb others.
  • There is a 10 minute break after each sitting.
  • Please do not enter or leave the meditation hall during the Group Sitting periods, or when tapes of Goenka chanting or giving instructions are being played.
  • Part-time sitting is an option. Please plan to arrive or leave at the lunch break if sitting part-time.

Location

The One-Day Courses will be held at the Seattle Vipassana Hall, which is located at 1800 Westlake Ave. N, Suite #301, Seattle, WA 98109 (view map).

Dhamma Service

If you are interested in helping with the setup or take-down of a one-day course, or if you would like more information about giving Dhamma service, please email info-seattle@kunja.dhamma.org.
